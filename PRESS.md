# Press & Media Information

## Project Name
**Vector Helper**

## Short Description
Vector Helper is a conceptual AI-driven accessibility assistant designed to
reduce cognitive overload and support users through contextual, ethical, and
human-centered guidance.

The project explores how artificial intelligence, wearable devices, and
accessibility-first design principles can converge into a future assistive
ecosystem.

---

## Project Status
Vector Helper is a **conceptual and research-oriented project**.

It is not a commercial product and is not currently implemented as a functional
device or software platform.

All materials published in this repository represent architectural vision,
ethical frameworks, and system-level design thinking.

---

## Purpose & Motivation
The project was created to:
- Explore new approaches to accessibility and assistive AI
- Address cognitive overload in modern digital environments
- Encourage ethical-by-design AI systems
- Inspire discussion among developers, researchers, and technology companies

---

## Key Focus Areas
- Accessibility-first system design
- Cognitive assistance and guidance
- Ethical AI constraints and safety laws
- Wearable and ambient computing concepts
- Human-centered interaction models
