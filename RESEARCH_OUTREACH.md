# Research Outreach & Intent

This document describes the intent, scope, and openness of the **Vector Helper / Visual Assistant Smart Glasses** project with respect to research, accessibility, and human–AI interaction communities.

It is not a commercial pitch, funding request, or product proposal.

---

## Purpose of This Document

The purpose of this document is to:

- Clarify the author’s intent behind publishing this project openly
- Indicate openness to academic, industrial, and independent research review
- Provide context for organizations exploring accessibility, cognitive assistance, and human–AI interfaces
- Preserve transparency regarding authorship, motivation, and limitations

---

## Project Nature

This project represents:

- A **conceptual and architectural framework**
- A **non-invasive cognitive assistance model**
- An **accessibility-first design philosophy**
- An **ethical and human-centered AI perspective**

It does **not** represent:
- A finished product
- A commercial offering
- A request for investment
- A request for exclusive partnership

---

## Intended Audience

This repository may be of interest to:

- Accessibility research teams
- Human–computer interaction (HCI) researchers
- Assistive technology developers
- AI ethics and alignment researchers
- Industrial research labs exploring cognitive augmentation
- Independent researchers and students

---

## Openness to Research Engagement

The author welcomes:

- Conceptual review and critique
- Ethical discussion
- Architectural comparison
- Independent research references
- Non-binding dialogue around similar problem spaces

No obligation, expectation, or response is implied or required.

## Ethical Boundary

Any future use, reference, or inspiration drawn from this project should:

- Respect accessibility-first principles
- Avoid exploitative or manipulative applications
- Consider cognitive load, autonomy, and user consent
- Align with the ethical framework described in `ETHICS.md` and `LAWS.md`
